# Setup Wizard

**Start here.** Before using any skills, run the setup wizard to configure this toolkit for your salons & spas business.

## How to use

1. Open `setup-wizard.md` in your AI assistant (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.)
2. The AI will walk you through a series of questions about your business
3. Answer each question — the more detail you provide, the better your skills will work
4. The wizard produces a `config.yml` file — save it to the root of this repo

## What it asks

The wizard covers:

- **Company basics** — name, location, service area, years in business
- **Team structure** — size, roles, who handles what
- **Tools & software** — CRM, scheduling, invoicing, communication tools
- **Pricing & rates** — your rate structure, typical job sizes, payment terms
- **Services & specialties** — what you offer, what you're known for
- **Communication style** — formal vs. casual, brand voice, how you talk to customers
- **Pain points** — what takes the most time, where you lose money

## Example output

See `config.example.yml` for what a completed configuration looks like.

## Re-running the wizard

You can re-run the wizard anytime. Just open `setup-wizard.md` again and tell the AI you want to update your configuration. It will load your existing `config.yml` and walk you through changes.
